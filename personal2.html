Welcome to my personal page! Thanks for taking the time to learn a little bit about me.

My name's Taylor. I was born and raised in Washington D.C. and I am currently living in Pittsburgh, PA. I graduated from Pitt with the class of 2013, double majoring in Philosophy and Political Science with a minor in Studio Arts.

I'm fascinated with understanding how things work. I'm a deductive learner, and only feel that I really understand something when I know it from top to bottom. One of my favorite projects as a kid was assembling and building a computer from scratch with my dad (quad core intel water cooled w/ 2x 750 MB vid boards in SLI, 2x 150 GB WD raptors w/ RAID 0 config and 2x 150 MB Corsair memory sticks).

I like movies with subtitles, painting in my spare time, traveling and experiencing new cultures, and reading TechCrunch religiously.

Having already experienced starting my own company, my ultimate goal is to start one again within the next 4 years. I'm attracted to tech because of the speed at which it can bring truly valuable products to market, and I'm excited to be able to contribute something as well.
